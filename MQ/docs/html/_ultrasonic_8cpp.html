◆ MicroSecondsToCentimeters()

long MicroSecondsToCentimeters ( long  travelTime)

Converts the passed time of flight to centimeters

Explanation:
Sound travels 1000 meters in 2.9 seconds
It should travel 1000/2.9 meters in 1 second

(1000/2.9) * time = distance travelled in that time (m/s)
To convert to centimeters/microseconds, multiply by 100, then divide by (10e6)
[ (1000*100)/(2.9 * 100000 * 10) ] * time
(1/29) * time
Since the measured time is for going and returning, we divide by 2
