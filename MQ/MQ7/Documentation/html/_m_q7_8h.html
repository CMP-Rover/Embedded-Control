Class for MQ7 gas sensor.

This sensor detects the Carbon monoxide concentration and outputs it as a voltage signal from the analog pin. If the concentration is past a certain value (configured via the potentiometer on the back), it pulls the digital pin it has a range of 10 -> 500 ppm (parts per million)
